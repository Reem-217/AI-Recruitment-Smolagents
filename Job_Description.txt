Job Title: Medical Sales Specialist
Location: Remotely
Company Overview
IXIR PLUS is a leading provider of Herbal Supplements, dedicated to improving the health and well-being of our customers. We are committed to providing high-quality products and exceptional customer service.
Job Description
We are seeking a dynamic and skilled Medical Sales Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in both medical expertise and sales, with the ability to handle a high volume of patient interactions while maintaining professional and sensitive relationships.
Job Responsibilities
Provide medical consultation and support to patients, addressing their needs with empathy and professionalism.
Utilize strong sales techniques to understand patient concerns, highlight product benefits, handle objections, and close sales.
Build and maintain solid relationships with patients and healthcare professionals, ensuring trust and satisfaction.
Maintain a high standard of work quality, demonstrating a genuine willingness to assist patients.
Continuously evaluate and improve the quality of relationships built, ensuring long-term patient engagement and loyalty.
Lead and mentor a team of sales professionals, fostering a collaborative and high-performing environment.
Manage the sales cycle through CRM tools, ensuring accurate reporting and follow-ups.
Adapt to different patients' needs and situations quickly, demonstrating flexibility in approach.
Analyze sales data and patient feedback to identify trends and areas for improvement.
Commit to ongoing learning and professional development, staying updated with the latest medical advancements and market trends.
Experiment with A-B testing, MVP tests, and cohort testing to drive innovation and improvement.
Maintain strong negotiation skills to enhance negotiation positions while keeping customers extremely satisfied.
Skills and Qualifications
Bachelor's degree in pharmacy
Licensed pharmacist or doctor with relevant medical expertise.
Proven sales experience with strong listening, objection handling, and persuasion skills.
Excellent interpersonal skills, with the ability to build sensitive and trusting relationships.
Fluency in English, both written and spoken.
Proficiency with CRM tools and strong technological aptitude.
Strong negotiation skills and ability to highlight and sell opportunities.
Charming and respectful voice, with the ability to engage and influence customers effectively.
Strong mental resilience to handle the challenges and pressures of the role.
Compensation & Benefits
Monthly salary: 10,000 EGP.
Commission: 2% of total sales.
Opportunities for training and professional development.
Motivating and supportive work environment
Weekly Vacation policy:
Saturday and Sunday off  according to Dubai holiday
